Project Overview

Welcome to the Bracero Program Testimonies Analysis Project. This project aims to analyze two corpora of oral and written testimonies using various machine learning techniques: TF-IDF, Frequency Encoding, GloVe, Word2Vec and...

Data

We downloaded testimonies from the Bracero Oral History project from the University of Texas at El Paso, and the Bracero History Archive. The former comprised 29 second party testimonies, that is, testimonies told by bracero relatives or by former bracero employers; the latter... Members of the team cleaned and removed stopwords, and then proceeded to analyze the corpora on their own.

Analysis

Our sentiment analysis model achieved an accuracy of 85%. You can see more detailed results and visualizations in each of the following tabs: TF-IDF and Frequency Encoding, GloVe and Word2Vec, and...
